WHAT IS MEGA

MEGA is a secure cloud storage and file sharing service at mega.nz. It was founded in 2013 and is based in Auckland New Zealand. MEGA uses end to end encryption so files are encrypted on your device before uploading. MEGA has no advertisements on its download pages. MEGA free accounts give 20 GB of storage but limit downloads to about 5 GB every 6 hours. This is called the transfer quota. MEGA is also called MEGA.nz or mega nz. Many Minecraft creators use MEGA to share addons, mods, and worlds because the interface is clean and there are no fake download buttons. Revenue comes from paid plans, not ads.

HOW TO DOWNLOAD FROM MEGA

1. Open the MEGA link. The URL starts with mega.nz/file/ or mega.nz/folder/.
2. The page shows the file name and file size. Check that this matches what you expected.
3. Click the red Download button. Your browser will start downloading the encrypted file data. A progress bar appears.
4. After the encrypted data is downloaded, your browser will decrypt the file locally. You will see a decrypting message. This uses JavaScript and needs no extra software. Large files take longer to decrypt.
5. After decryption, your browser will ask where to save the finished file. Choose your Downloads folder and save.
6. For Minecraft files like .mcpack .mcaddon .mcworld, tap the file to auto import. For .jar files, place it in your .minecraft/mods folder.
7. If the link is a folder with multiple files, you can download individual files or use Download as ZIP to get everything at once.
8. Instead of downloading, you can click Import to Cloud Drive if you have a free MEGA account. The file saves to your own MEGA storage and you can download it later without transfer quota limits.

COMMON PROBLEMS

PROBLEM: Transfer quota exceeded error appears.
SOLUTION: You have downloaded about 5 GB in the past 6 hours. Wait for the quota to reset or switch to a different network like mobile data. You can also create a free MEGA account and import the file to your cloud drive, then download it from there.

PROBLEM: Download is stuck on Decrypting for a long time.
SOLUTION: Large files need more time to decrypt in your browser. Be patient. If it takes more than 15 minutes, close the tab and try again with the MEGA desktop app or MEGA mobile app instead.

PROBLEM: Download never starts or just spins.
SOLUTION: Try a Chromium based browser like Chrome, Edge, or Brave. Firefox and Safari sometimes have issues with MEGA streaming downloads. Check that your browser is not blocking pop ups for mega.nz.

PROBLEM: I am on iPhone or iPad and cannot download.
SOLUTION: Safari and Chrome on iOS limit downloads to about 1 MB. You must use the free MEGA iOS app from the App Store to download any Minecraft file. The app bypasses the browser limit.

PROBLEM: Download speed is very slow.
SOLUTION: You may have reached the transfer quota which triggers slower speeds. Wait 6 hours or switch networks. Peak internet congestion can also slow MEGA down.

PROBLEM: Link not found or file removed.
SOLUTION: The sharer may have deleted the file. Contact them for a new link. Ask in the MIA Discord if someone has an alternative.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS
