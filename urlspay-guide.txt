WHAT IS URLSPAY

Urlspay is a free URL shortener that pays creators per click. When you click a Urlspay link you must complete unlock rounds before reaching the final file. Each round has a timer, scrolling, and verification. Urlspay is similar to Linkvertise, Adfly, and Shorte.st. Urlspay has a low trust score of 47 out of 100 on Scamadviser. The domain was registered in July 2024. Many users report not receiving payouts. NOVA never shares Urlspay links. NOVA only uses direct download links with no ad walls.

HOW TO UNLOCK A URLSPAY LINK

1. Open the Urlspay link in Chrome or your device browser. Do not use the in app browser from apps like YouTube, Discord, or TikTok.
2. Wait a few seconds for the page to load. A Continue to Content button appears. Click it.
3. Wait about 20 seconds. A countdown timer may show. Do not close the tab or switch apps.
4. Tap Unlock Link when it becomes active. Scroll all the way to the bottom of the page.
5. Press the Verify button at the bottom. Wait for it to process. The button changes to Continue. Press Continue.
6. Repeat steps 3 to 5 from three to five times. Each round is the same: wait, unlock, scroll, verify, continue.
7. After the final round, press Unlock Link one more time. Scroll down, press Continue or Get Link.
8. You will be redirected to the actual file host, usually MediaFire, Google Drive, or MEGA.
9. Download your file from the final host page.

COMMON PROBLEMS

PROBLEM: The page keeps reloading or progress resets.
SOLUTION: Use Chrome or an external browser. In app browsers from Discord and YouTube break session tracking.

PROBLEM: The countdown timer keeps starting over.
SOLUTION: Stay on the page without switching tabs. Do not let your screen turn off.

PROBLEM: I cannot find the Verify button after scrolling.
SOLUTION: Scroll slowly to the very bottom. The button may be small or hidden below ads. Rotate your phone to landscape mode if on mobile.

PROBLEM: I completed many rounds but the link still does not unlock.
SOLUTION: Some links need up to seven rounds. If it still fails, the link may be broken. Ask the sharer for a direct link instead.

PROBLEM: A pop up warns my phone is infected with a virus.
SOLUTION: This is a fake ad. Close the tab immediately. Do not click any buttons on the pop up.

PROBLEM: A pop up asks to install a browser extension.
SOLUTION: Never install anything from link shortener pages. Close the tab. These are often malware.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS

· Never pay for any upgrade or premium service on Urlspay. It is a known scam risk.
· Never enter personal information, credit card details, or phone numbers.
· Never install browser extensions, apps, or software from Urlspay pages.
· Never click on banner ads or pop ups. Close unexpected tabs immediately.
· Ad blockers can break the unlock process. Disable them temporarily then re enable after.
· Urlspay has cryptocurrency services flagged as high risk. Avoid any crypto prompts.
· Use Chrome or Safari for best compatibility. In app browsers cause failures.
